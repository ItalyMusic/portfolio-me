Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with MGN STUDIO

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A typical website design takes 4-6 weeks, mobile app designs 6-8 weeks, and comprehensive branding projects 8-12 weeks. We always provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide and are experienced in managing projects across different time zones. Our communication is key, and we ensure regular updates regardless of location. We've successfully completed projects for clients in over 15 countries.

What's included in your design process?

Our comprehensive process includes research and discovery, wireframing and prototyping, visual design with glassmorphism effects, interactive prototypes, and delivery of final assets. We also provide detailed style guides, design systems, and collaborate with your development team to ensure perfect implementation.

How do you handle revisions?

We include up to 3 rounds of revisions in all our projects. Additional revisions can be accommodated at an hourly rate. We believe in getting it right and ensuring client satisfaction. Our revision process is structured to be efficient while maintaining the highest quality standards.

What makes glassmorphism special?

Glassmorphism creates depth, elegance, and modern sophistication in digital interfaces. It uses transparency and blur effects to create layered experiences that feel both premium and intuitive. This design trend is particularly effective for creating visual hierarchy and drawing attention to key content.
